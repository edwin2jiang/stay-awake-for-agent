## Style Prompt

Midnight utility launch film for a macOS menu bar app built for long-running AI agents. The visual world comes from the existing app icon and hero banner: deep night canvas, teal power rings, cyan screen glow, small moonlight details, and a warm amber bolt as the action signal. The tone is calm, technical, and quietly premium: a machine staying awake through the night without looking frantic.

## Colors

- `#080F18` — midnight base
- `#101D2A` — elevated app surface
- `#2AE6C8` — active awake / power pulse
- `#A7FFF0` — high-contrast glow text
- `#FFCC58` — caution, battery, lightning accent
- `#F6F8EF` — moonlit primary text

## Typography

- Display and Chinese UI: `Noto Sans JP`, with heavy weights for launch-film scale text
- Technical labels and timers: `IBM Plex Mono`, `JetBrains Mono`, monospace

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use generic blue SaaS gradients or purple launch-video colors.
- Do not make the app feel loud, gamer-like, or security-alarm aggressive.
- Do not hide the actual product behind abstract neon only; show the app icon and UI behavior.
- Do not use beige, brown, or warm editorial palettes outside the amber accent.
- Do not use jump cuts; transitions should feel like a machine passing a stable signal forward.
